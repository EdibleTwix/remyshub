How To Apply

Things You Should Know

  • Read and accept the server's rules here to be accepted into a squadron.

  • Discord members have priority. You will need to be verified after joining the Discord server.

  • You server display name or nickname needs to be changed to your War Thunder in-game username.

  • Submit an application to apply to a squadron. We (soon will) accept applicants applying through this website. We do not accept people applying through Direct Messages (DMs).

  • It may take up to a week or more to be accepted. This could be the result of the squadron reaching full member capacity, a high number or demand of applicants, or an issue with your application. At the time being, you can either wait for an available spot or join one of our brother squadrons.

  • Feel free to contact us here if you have any issues or questions!
